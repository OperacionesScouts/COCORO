# **App Name**: COCORO

## Core Features:

- User Registration: A publicly accessible form for event participants to register their details including personal information (Nombre y Apellido, Número de Cedula, Correo, Grupo Scout), payment information (Referencia bancaría, Fecha de pago, Monto), and to securely upload payment proof (Adjuntar comprobante).
- Payment & User Validation (Admin): An administrative interface allowing designated users to review submitted registrations, verify uploaded payment proofs against bank references and amounts, and update the payment status to 'validated'.
- Unique QR Code Generation: Upon successful payment validation by an administrator, a unique, scannable QR code is automatically generated for the registered participant and linked to their profile.
- Attendance QR Scanner (Operations): A mobile-friendly interface for Operations staff to scan participant QR codes via their phone cameras to quickly and accurately record event attendance at entry points.
- Payment Status & QR Inquiry: A public portal where registered participants can enter their Número de Cedula to instantly check their payment validation status and securely view or download their unique event QR code.
- AI-Powered Payment Receipt Analysis: An AI tool for administrators to automatically extract key details like payment reference, amount, and date from uploaded payment receipts, flagging any discrepancies with registered data to streamline the verification process.
- Role-Based Access Control: Secure authentication and authorization system to differentiate access levels for public users (registrants), administrators, and operations staff.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, authoritative blue (#0B305F) reminiscent of the Scouts brand, conveying trust and professionalism.
- Background color: A clean, light gray (#F7F7F7) providing a neutral and spacious canvas that enhances content readability, aligning with modern web design.
- Accent color: A vibrant gold-yellow (#FDCB01) to highlight important actions, interactive elements, and key information, drawing inspiration from the Scouts visual identity.
- Headline and body font: 'Open Sans' (sans-serif) for its versatility, excellent readability, and professional appearance, consistent with the clean aesthetic of the Scouts website.
- Use clean, functional vector icons (e.g., checkmarks, QR code symbols, upload icons) that align with the straightforward and community-focused visual language of the Scouts, providing clear visual cues.
- Employ a responsive design approach with clear, hierarchical layouts. The registration form is concise, admin dashboards prioritize data overview and actionability, and the operations interface is optimized for mobile QR scanning, all ensuring usability across devices and echoing the structured approach of the Scouts website.
- Incorporate subtle and swift feedback animations for user interactions, such as form submissions, successful QR scans, or status updates, to provide a modern and engaging user experience without being obtrusive.
